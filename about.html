Discovery

First, we will sit down and discuss your project in depth. Once we understand your business, your users and case scenarios, we will create characters for each type of user and we will start to establish requirements. Finally, we will write user stories to start our process.

Design Thinking

When our UX team has a knowledge depth of the task at hand, we will begin to create detailed architecture diagrams describing how users will interact with your application. These details serve as a roadmap for design, the high-level interactions, page elements and hierarchy. At the end of this stage we present a prototype in high definition.

Development | SCRUM

As soon as the customer has approved the models interaction and aesthetic design of the product We will start with development. As we move forward, we discover and we solve the problems early in collaboration with our clients' teams.

Every two weeks, we organize meetings sprint planning to assign tasks to specific engineers. The goal is to have a collection of features completed at the end of each sprint. Which will be presented to the client for get your approval and feedback.

UAT & Launch

Before publishing your application, we will take you through of a final acceptance testing process with real users to ensure that the complete system achieves your business objective. Once finished We will publish your application and deliver the operation manuals as well as installation.
